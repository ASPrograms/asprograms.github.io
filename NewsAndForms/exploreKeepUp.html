The Keep Up Service

A simple and clean notification bus, that keeps you informed about our latest updates, via the mode you choose.

Various modes of notifications are :

  • • Email
  • • Discord
  • • Whatsapp
  • • Short Message Service

What info do we use?

We use your IP Address, for security purposes. This is to ensure that no other person can fill the form or get enrolled with us with no consent, in unauthorised way.

We may need to store your mail while you subscribe the service, in order to avoid multi-response conflicts and to send notifications.

Is my info safe with ASPrograms?

The info that we receive from our subscribers, is stored in well-encrypted form and we ownselves even don't access the info as long as we don't face strong technical issues or requirements. Our automated notification bus implicitly access the information and has no external execution.
