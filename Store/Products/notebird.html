App Info

NoteBird is a project distributed in the form of an app as well as a web-app ( under development ) that aims making note-taking easier, more productive, more secure, more personalised and most importantly, more organised.
The usage of this app is free up to 1 GB Storage of your notes. As for now, we have developed it for 1GB max storage, would soon implement more storage.

We store on our server, but don't access your information
